Devta Sloka

ॐ शिरोमेश्रीर्यशोमुखंत्विषिः केशाश्चश्मश्रूणि राजामेप्राणोऽअमृतं सम्राट् चक्षुर्विराटश्रोत्रम्।। ॐभू भो रोग इहा रोगाय नमः।।

Devta Description

Mrityu was born off the wedlock of Bhaya and Maya who belonged to the Dharma clan which emerged out of Prajapati Daksha Mrityu has five sons - Vyadhi, Jara, Shoka, Trishna and Krodha. Vyadhi is synonymous with Roga and occupies the north west pada in the Vaastu chakra. He is the most frightful of the Gods and grants death and bondage Angering him results in death.
